About us

At Pawsitive Change

we believe every animal deserves a loving home. Our mission is to simplify animal adoption in Tunisia by creating a dedicated platform that connects families and shelters.

By showcasing adoptable animals, reuniting lost pets with their owners, and promoting responsible pet ownership, we aim to reduce the number of stray animals and the associated health risks.

Together, we’re building a compassionate community where every animal has a chance to thrive in a safe and caring environment.
